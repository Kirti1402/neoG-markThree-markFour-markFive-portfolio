Mysore Trip

Mysore is the second largest city in the state of Karnataka, India.According to Hindu mythology, the area around Mysore was known as Mahishuru and was ruled by a demon, Mahishasura. The demon was killed by the Goddess Chamundeshwari, whose temple is situated atop the Chamundi Hills.

5 Best place to Visit in Mysore

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace. It is a historical palace. It is main attraction of Mysore City and situated in center of city. You can visit at evening also to see the lightning from outside. Opening time is from 10:00 Am to 5:00 PM, Ticket: 200 Rs/ per person.

Chamundi Hills

Chamundi Hill name come frome Chamundeshwari temple at the peak.You can experience a beautiful city view from hill. Travelers can visit Chamundi hills any hour of the day, however it is preferable not to visit it after sunset as it might get unsafe. The timings for the Chamundeshwari Temple is from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM

Opening timing: 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM(Safe to visit in day time)
Tickets: NA

Sand Museum

Sand Museum is on the way to chamundihill. It is a compact museum devoted to sand art, housing several intricate sculptures.

Opening timing: 8:00 am to 5:00pm
Ticket: Rs 60 for adult & Rs 40 for childer

Sea Shell Museum

Guinness World Record Sea Shell Art Museum located near Chamundi Hills just 3.5 KM from Mysore Palace. museum of beautiful artwork, sculptures and the magnificent recreation of Taj Mahal in seashells.

Opening timing: 9:00 am to 5:00pm
Ticket: Rs 60 for adult & Rs 40 for childer
